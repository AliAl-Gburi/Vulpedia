About us

Who we are

Our team

We are a team of 4 zoologists trying to share their passion for foxes. For the past 10 years, our team has scoured the globe to study foxes. Our team is composed of different strengths, we are all zoologists, however we specialize in different things.

Our mission

Our plan is to make information about foxes to be as widely available as possible. We believe that foxes must be appreciated by as many people as possible. In order to fulfill our mission:

  • We plan on making this website intuitive and easy to use.
  • We also plan on making Vulpedia light weight and accessible on smartphones.
  • Our final task in our mission is to translate the articles to multiple languages.
